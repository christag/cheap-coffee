# Starbucks drinks in February 2026: recipes, customizations, and complete specifications

The Starbucks menu for February 2026 features the winter pistachio lineup alongside new Dubai Chocolate limited-time offerings and Valentine's Day specials. The free non-dairy milk policy (since November 2024) remains in effect, and a standardized flat $0.80 modifier fee (since June 2025) makes multi-syrup hacks more economical. Condiment bars were fully restored in January 2025 as part of the "Back to Starbucks" initiative. Prices have increased approximately 3-6% since September 2025.

## Winter 2026 menu features pistachio, Dubai Chocolate, and Valentine's specials

The current seasonal menu launched January 6, 2026 and centers around pistachio flavors. The **Pistachio Latte** returns as a seasonal favorite with pistachio sauce and salted brown butter cookie topping, available hot and iced. The **Pistachio Cream Cold Brew** layers cold brew with vanilla syrup and pistachio sweet cream cold foam. New for winter 2026, the **Pistachio Cortado** offers the pistachio profile in an 8-ounce format with three ristretto shots.

The **Dubai Chocolate** limited-time offerings launched alongside the winter menu: **Iced Dubai Chocolate Matcha** combines matcha with pistachio sauce and chocolate cream cold foam, while the **Iced Dubai Chocolate Mocha** features espresso with mocha sauce and pistachio cream cold foam.

For Valentine's Day (launched February 3, 2026), the **White Chocolate Strawberry Cream Cold Brew** pairs cold brew with white chocolate strawberry cream cold foam and chocolate curls, and the **Strawberry Shortcake Frappuccino** blends strawberry puree with cinnamon dolce syrup.

New permanent additions include **Iced Double Berry Matcha** (strawberry puree layered with matcha and raspberry cream cold foam) and **Iced Banana Bread Matcha** (matcha with brown sugar syrup and banana cream cold foam).

All fall 2025 seasonal drinks (Pumpkin Spice Latte, Pecan Oatmilk Cortado, Pumpkin Cream Cold Brew, etc.) were removed when the winter menu launched.

## Standard recipes follow precise pump and shot patterns

Barista training materials reveal the exact specifications for every drink size. Hot beverages receive **2/3/4/5 pumps** of syrup for short (8oz), tall (12oz), grande (16oz), and venti (20oz) respectively, paired with **1/1/2/2 espresso shots**. Iced drinks follow a different pattern with **3/4/6/7 pumps** for tall through trenta (30oz) sizes and **1/2/3 shots** for tall through venti iced. The distinction exists because venti iced cups hold 24 ounces versus 20 ounces for venti hot, requiring additional syrup and espresso to maintain flavor balance against ice dilution.

The pump systems themselves differ significantly: hot bar pumps dispense **½ ounce** (1 tablespoon) per pump, while cold bar pumps deliver **¼ ounce** (½ tablespoon). Brown sugar syrup uses special half-dose pumps, effectively halving the sweetness compared to standard syrups. Mocha and white mocha sauces use the same ½-ounce pumps as hot bar syrups.

## Standardized modifier pricing simplifies customization costs (June 2025)

On June 24, 2025, Starbucks standardized modifier pricing with a **flat $0.80 fee** for adding ANY combination of syrups and/or sauces to an unflavored drink. This is per-drink, not per-syrup -- whether you add 1 syrup or 5, it's one flat $0.80 charge. This is a significant improvement for hack strategies that combine multiple syrups.

Key pricing rules:
- **Flat $0.80** for any syrup/sauce additions to unflavored drinks
- **FREE syrup substitution** on pre-flavored drinks (swap vanilla for caramel, etc.)
- **FREE classic syrup** added to any drink
- **FREE extra pumps** of the same syrup
- **$1.00/scoop** for matcha powder added to non-matcha drinks
- **$0.50/scoop** for dried fruit additions
- **$0.80** for extra chai concentrate
- **Non-dairy milks** (oat, almond, soy, coconut) remain FREE

## Free milk alternatives and condiment bar restored

The November 7, 2024 policy eliminating non-dairy milk upcharges remains fully in effect, saving customers up to $0.80 per drink. **Oat, almond, soy, and coconut milk** substitutions are completely free.

The self-service condiment bar was **fully restored** on January 27, 2025 as part of the "Back to Starbucks" initiative. Available items include creamers, milk, and sweeteners. Free powder toppings include **cinnamon and cocoa powder**. Nutmeg powder was discontinued.

**Free refills** of hot/iced brewed coffee or tea are now available to ALL customers (not just Rewards members) when ordering "for here" in a ceramic mug, glass, or personal reusable cup.

## Menu simplification continues with 30% reduction target

Starbucks continues its menu simplification initiative. **13 drinks were discontinued on March 4, 2025**, including several Frappuccino varieties, Iced Matcha Lemonade, White Hot Chocolate, Royal English Breakfast Latte, and Honey Almondmilk Flat White. More cuts are planned to reach the 30% total reduction target.

New permanent additions since September 2025:
- **Pistachio sauce** is now available year-round (previously seasonal only)
- **Raspberry syrup** returned permanently (reformulated without artificial colors)
- **Sugar-Free Caramel syrup** added as permanent option
- **Caramel Protein Latte** and **Caramel Protein Matcha** (protein-boosted milk, 27-31g protein)
- **Iced Double Berry Matcha** and **Iced Banana Bread Matcha** (permanent matcha variations)

## Rewards program overhauled (March 10, 2026)

Starbucks Rewards restructured into 3 tiers: Green (entry), Gold (500 Stars/year), and Reserve (2,500 Stars/year). New features include:
- **60 Stars** = $2 off any purchase (new lower redemption tier)
- **Free Mod Mondays**: one free customization per month for all members
- Gold/Reserve members earn bonus stars (1.2x and 1.7x respectively)

## Spring 2026 preview (launching March 3, 2026)

Announced but not yet available: Toasted Coconut Cream Cold Brew, Toasted Coconut Latte, Iced Lavender Cream Chai, Lavender Latte (returning), Iced Lavender Cream Matcha (returning), Iced Ube Coconut Macchiato (limited time), and customizable Energy Refreshers.

## Price increases of 3-6% since September 2025

Starbucks implemented price increases of approximately 3-6% on handcrafted beverages in late 2025/early 2026:
- Basic brewed coffee: ~$0.20 increase
- Espresso drinks: ~$0.30 increase
- Cold brew/nitro: ~$0.30-$0.50 increase
- Specialty/seasonal drinks: ~$0.30-$0.50 increase

Prices vary significantly by location (up to $2.50 difference between markets).
